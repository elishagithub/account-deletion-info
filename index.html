Request to Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

Steps to Delete Your Account:

1. Open the app after successfully signed in and click on the Profile a bottom nav bar item.
2. Scroll to the bottom of the page and Select "Delete Account" and follow the prompts.
3. Confirm your account deletion.

Data Deletion Policy:

When you delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed from our servers.
This includes your profile information, order history, and any preferences you have set.
Data that may be retained (if any) will follow legal and compliance requirements.

Contact Us:

If you need assistance with this process, please contact our support team at suresh@farmtohome.live.
